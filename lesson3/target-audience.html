Target Audience

My target audience for this website will be adults, age 25-50, who are small business owners. Additional site content will be geared toward people who may be visiting our locale.

Persona 1- Jane Johnson

I'm 35 yers old, and I run a small tea shop in Machias area. I am looking for ways to connect with other small business owners. I'd like to spread the word about my shop, and feel like the Chamber of Commerce might be a good place to start.
How do I join?
How do I connect with other business owners?
Will I get notifications from the Chamber?

Persona 2- Sam Smith

I'm 43 years old, and my family and I are visiting Washington State soon. I am wondering what sort of things there are to do with my kids.
Are there restaurants?
Shops?
Recreational Areas?
Fill me in about your town!

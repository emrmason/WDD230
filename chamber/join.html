Membership Selection

Membership Options:

Non-Profit (Annual Cost: None)

Specifically designed with our non-profit business owners in mind, this membership is free of charge. Included in this membership are:

  • One annual training
  • Quarterly advertising in local mailers
  • Booth provided at local farmer's market for 6 selected weeks of the season

Bronze Membership (Annual Cost: $500)

We love our Bronze Business Members! Here are the benefits of this paid membership:

  • Two annual trainings
  • Quarterly advertising in local mailers
  • Booth provided at local farmer's market for 8 selected weeks of the season

Silver Membership (Annual Cost: $1,000)

Silver Business Members get all the benefits of the Bronze membership, plus these additional services:

  • One half page advertising space in our annual calendars sent to local residents
  • Advertising as a sponsor of a local community sports team

Gold Membership (Annual Cost: $1,500)

Gold Business Members add all of these services with their package purchase:

  • Advertising as a sponsor of a local High School sports team
  • Monthly mail advertisements to local residents
  • Booth provided at local farmer's market for entire season
